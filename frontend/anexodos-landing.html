Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI solutions

What makes Omnix AI different from other AI assistants?

Omnix AI combines browser automation, voice-to-voice conversations, and advanced reasoning in one platform. With both Flash (for quick tasks) and Maxima (for deep thinking) models, it adapts to your specific needs while maintaining conversation context across different modes.

Is my data secure with Anéxodos AI?

Yes, we prioritize data security and privacy. All interactions are encrypted, and we follow international data protection standards. Your personal information and learning data are never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I use these AI tools offline?

Currently, our AI solutions require an internet connection to access the latest models and real-time capabilities. However, we're working on offline modes for basic features that will be available in future updates.

How can I get started with Anéxodos AI?

Getting started is easy! For Apola AI, visit apolaai.com and create your student account. For Omnix AI, click the "Get Started" button above to access the platform. Both offer intuitive interfaces and guided onboarding to help you get the most out of our AI tools.
